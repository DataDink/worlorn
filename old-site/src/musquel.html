musquel-by-the-sea
ko’s home, abandoned, destroyed, annihilated







It is known that Musquel-by-the-Sea was a turf in the Cycle of Dreams dominated by Ko.

It is said that the Dark Horse Typhon Annihilated the wasteland of Musquel-by-the-Sea after the Destruction of Ko.

It is demonstrable that there is a crater on the boundary of Troad where Ko’s Musquel used to be, and that Dhruva Preserved the lands as Musquel-in-the-Abyss.

It is known that Ko has not appeared in the Cycle of Dance.
