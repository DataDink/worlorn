los endos
the end of the world








los endos is an island off the northern coast of troad. it has been the north pole in certain Times, as well as being a dragon isle for all Times.
there are three mountains and a jade house. the book of skulls may be invoked at the jade house to withhold termination of life from natural causes (ageless immortality). there are monks who can show you how it's done. be certain to be in a party of four. it's essential.
lat the end of the road is xcalis regence's strange blue dome.







sheherezade
who's in charge here








sheherezade is the kwisach haderach, an abomination, a perfect combination of genetic heritage from the five founding families of worlorn (troy, ko, llewellyn, yama, knock) and the poets of the first five cycles of worlorn (comedy, astronomy, history, dreams, and dance).
her lucky number is 5.
sheherezade is the daughter of xcalis regence and dira.
