the high one
worlorn's founder







the high one is the organizer of oimota's 16 dominators. she is striving to sustain the inspiration of the muse of dreams into the cycle of dance.
to this end she wields oimota, the crown of worlorn; the codex of the infinite planes; and the red shoes. her weapons are the pattern of troy, and a peace treaty.
evidence suggests she's troy ounces, the half elven one.
