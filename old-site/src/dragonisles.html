The Dragon Isles
Here there be Dragons...








The Dragon Isles are an archipelago in the Watersea of Worlorn. The shipping lanes of the Phoenecian Elfar and the Islanders steer clear of these islands, when they can. Tales tell of at least eight such islands known to have been visited by the brave or foolish. Three, Elidor, Los Endos, and Mestea, have dominators of their own, but the others must be assumed to be home to Dragons alone.







Styphon
Emissary of Shal-Omnis








Styphon is a sentient dragon who's goal and pleasure is to thwart Shal Omis, and to Dance in Worlorn, preventing Her Arrival. Styphon also appears in the legends of Niteblade
