Darkness








Darkness is an evil dragon.








Night, Dragons, Faeries








Darkness is the deity of night, assassins, the moons, faeries, and dragons.








Law and Evil








Followers of Darkness tend to be evil, favoring organized groups.








Dark Side/Bright Side








When the Faerie town of Kendol was turned to Blood by Dira, Titania moved the high altar of Darkness to the Dark Side of the Moon. After an alteracation with a monster cursed to be there, she moved the high altar to the Bright Side.








Titania

Faeries
