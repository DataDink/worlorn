almitra almustafa
The Summoner, Bitch That Broke da World








Almitra appears as a caucasian female with jet black hair and electric blue eyes. She stands a slim 5’ 4”, but with attitude. She rules Llewghund proximately, often allowing dreamers to summon her attention or avatar.
For someone with the will to freeze flux into irridisium (or anything else she whims) she comes across as light hearted and capricious. Her wide ranging interests are evidenced throughout Llewghund as well as in her personal fashion statements.
Almitra is said to have seduced the Phoenix ”Threnody” to obtain the Light of the World. She is said to have seduced the dream of Troy Ounces, The Half Elven One, to father her second son, Simon Almustafa. She is said to have seduced the Great Mage Salt Peter into sharing his place of Power, Port Suame, for Simon to inherit.
It is said that Almitra turned an invited guest to Llewghund, the Worlorn kwisatz haderach, to Blood. However, from all accounts of the great bard Halifirien Wedge, Almitra is a most welcoming and attentive hostess to Inspired guests.
