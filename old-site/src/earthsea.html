earthsea
the Undesignated Wastelands of Roke







Earthsea is wasteland sustained by the Masters at Roke. These wastelands are not included in Turf or Wastelands of Oimota’s 16 Dominators in any timeframe.

  1. Ogygia, Island of St Kalia.
  2. Corum’s Isle, Island of Corum Jhalien Irsei
  3. Northlands, a place of warlike countries
  4. South Pole, a place of red dragons with Roland’s tomb.
  5. North Pole, Dominated by the 8 ball of Xcalis Regence I
  6. Melnibone, the Dreaming Isle.
  7. Voidcastle, an asteroid in the void
  8. Amphitheatre, a place from the Cycle of Comedy
  9. Gateway, an asteroid in the void
  10. Darkside, dark half of the moon, Dominated by Jormundgand,
  11. Brightside, light half of the moon, Dominated by Ourobouros
  12. Carsultyl, a volcanic jungle island, contested by Drow factions
