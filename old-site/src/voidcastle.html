voidcastle
home of terpsichore?








Voidcastle is an emerald city with the same design as Diaspar, Ilium, Muili, New Troia, Rebma, and Troy.
It is said to have been created by Vlad Drakulya Tepes, but others claim that his lifelong friend Remo created it. It is suggested that when Vlad annihilated Castle Drakulya while misusing a Slaver weapon, he found himself stranded in the Void with Remo. Remo’s half-dragon intellect used a Major Arcana Trump card to form a city ex nihilum, out of the Void, around the lost pair. the Voidcastle city allowed the pair to recover from the mind-numbing vacancy of Greyspace, so the lost pair was able to rest sufficiently until a way back to Worlorn could be found.
